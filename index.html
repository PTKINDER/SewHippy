What I do

Welcome to Sew Hippy, where I make all sorts of hippy and bohemian style wears and trinkets.

I like to put my hand to anything, so let me know if you have an idea and I will see if I can accommodate.

Dressmaking

I make a variety of childrens clothing, including dresses, skirts, tutus and shorts. All made to order.

I can also alter dresses to let in or out. If you need any assistance, I'm your gal!

Jewellery & Trinkets

Everything from necklaces, rings and earrings. I use things I've collected on my wanders, such as sea glass, tumbled pottery and pressed flowers. I love to seek out these little treasures and set them in resin to preserve their beauty.

Crafts

I love being crafty! Check out my personalised keepsake frames, homemade candles and bespoke hair bow organisers!

Please email for prices or if you require a customised item.
